STD Symptoms for Girls
Chlamydia Most women do not experience any symptoms. some experience vaginal discharge, abdominal pain. It can cause severe pelvic infection, which can lead to infertility and tubal pregnancy.
Gonorrhea Burning or frequent urination, a yellowish vaginal discharge, redness and swelling of the genitals, and a burning or itching of the vaginal area.
HPV Appearance of a genital lesion. Genital warts usually appear as small, fleshy, raised bumps on sexually-exposed area and are sometimes associated with itching, burning, or tenderness in the area.
Genital herpes Initial itching or tingling sensation followed by redness of the skin. Then a blister forms, followed by ulcers when the blisters break.
